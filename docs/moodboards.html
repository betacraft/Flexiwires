Rules of the road

Do this. Never that.

→ Do
  • Hairline 1px borders everywhere. No drop shadows on dark.
  • Reserve the mint for live data and the primary CTA.
  • Use mono for numbers, FX rates, IDs, timestamps.
  • Negative space is content. Don't fill it.
  • One serif-italic word per heading, max — editorial spice.
  • Sound like an engineer wrote the copy — precise, dry, confident.
↛ Don't
  • No neon purples, web3 grids, or "cyber" aesthetics.
  • No gradients on body text or buttons.
  • No emojis. No mascots. No illustrated humans.
  • No glassmorphism on every card — it ruins the discipline.
  • No mid-grey on dark backgrounds. Always check WCAG AA.
  • No marketing exclamations. No "amazing." No "powerful."

§ 05 · Principles · the WHY

Five named rules. Memorise them.

Five named rules that govern the Obsidian system. Every component traces back to one of these.

01

Glow on live data only.

Mint accent is reserved for the primary CTA and real-time signals — rates, "LIVE" badges, status dots. Decorating with mint dilutes its meaning. When every card glows, nothing signals.

02

Hairline before shadow.

1px ivory/8% borders carry hierarchy on dark. Drop-shadows kill the institutional feel. If a card needs to "lift," tighten the border opacity instead of adding shadow.

03

Numbers in mono, always.

FX rates, IDs, timestamps, account numbers — JetBrains Mono tabular figures. Decimals align across rows. The product page reads like a trading terminal because it acts like one.

04

One italic word per heading.

Instrument Serif italic accents exactly one editorial word per display heading. More than one cheapens the move. The italic word is the emotional word.

05

Sound like an engineer.

Precise, dry, confident copy. No exclamation marks. No "amazing." No "powerful." If the product can't earn the verb, don't use it. CFOs don't buy adjectives.

§ 05 · Principles · the WHY

Five named rules. Memorise them.

Five named rules that govern Lumen Rails. Editorial restraint over visual abundance.

01

One prism per page.

The rainbow gradient is a hero anchor — it never repeats inside a page, never decorates a card, never lives on a button. If it shows up twice, it stops being premium.

02

Hairlines, never shadows.

Edge-color 1px borders separate every surface. Cards lift on border colour change, not on shadow elevation. Shadows are an FT-page sin in this system.

03

Tabular by default.

Every number in JetBrains Mono with tabular figures. Decimals align in tables. The body type is Inter; the data type is mono. They never trade places.

04

Indigo is action.

The accent fires on Primary CTAs and "best rate" highlights — that's it. Ink does headings. Edge does separation. Muted does support. Indigo only converts.

05

Mobile-first leading.

Body copy stays at 1.55–1.6 line-height on every breakpoint. Tight prose works on a laptop and breaks on a phone. We design for the phone.

Rules of the road

Do this. Never that.

→ Do
  • Cabinet Grotesk for display, never anywhere else.
  • Tuned voltage lime for primary CTAs · bronze for Enterprise.
  • Warm shadows (warm-tinted, low opacity) not cool grey ones.

§ 05 · Principios · el PORQUÉ

Cinco reglas con nombre. Aprenderlas de memoria.

Cinco reglas con nombre que gobiernan el sistema Latin Voltage. Calidez sobre frialdad.

01

Bronce significa Enterprise.

El degradado metálico es una señal de premio, no decoración. Aparece en el plan Enterprise, en badges de cliente clave, y en transiciones de cierre. Si lo usas en otro lugar, pierde su voz.

02

Lidera el español, sigue el inglés.

Los titulares de landing van en español. Los subtítulos en inglés son opcionales — nunca al revés. Esta marca es de Latinoamérica, no para Latinoamérica.

03

Sombras cálidas, no frías.

Los tintes de sombra comparten la familia crema-bronce. Las sombras grises matan el calor. Si vas a elevar una superficie, hazlo con calor.

04

Las pastillas son herencia.

Los botones se quedan en 999px redondeados. La FlexiWires de 2025 ya enseñó esa pastilla — la rediseñamos brillante, no la rompemos.

05

Una palabra en cursiva por encabezado.

Instrument Serif italic acentúa exactamente una palabra emocional por encabezado. Cabinet Grotesk hace el peso; la cursiva pone el corazón.
