01 · Overview

Precise, fast, premium

FlexiWires compares every cross-border wire rail on one screen. The brand is engineered and calm with one decisive accent, a signature green that marks the moment of action. These three traits drive every decision in this document.

Engineered

Monospace data, exact figures, clean grids. It should feel built by people who move money.

Calm

Generous space, muted neutrals, a cream canvas. Green is rationed so it always means something.

Expressive

An italic serif accent line cuts through the sans headings, a human note in an engineered system.

Why this palette

Signature green (#3FE864) is reserved for action and confirmation. Green reads as go, success, money saved, exactly the feeling at send. Rationing it to CTAs, status dots and the live indicator keeps every green pixel meaningful, and black-on-green stays AA legible.

Cream and ink feel premium and editorial rather than clinical, and near-black softens harsh contrast for comfortable reading. Hero black (#111214) panels create rhythm and make the green pop hardest where it matters.

04 · Typography

Three typefaces

A neutral workhorse, an expressive accent, and a precise data face. Each role maps to a need a treasury product has: scanning, trust and emotion.

Compare rails, lock the rate
Inter · 300-700 · UI & headings
Why Inter

A screen-first neutral sans with a tall x-height, legible from 11px labels to 64px headlines. The same face works in marketing and in-app, so the landing page and product feel like one thing. Proven at interface scale across modern fintech and tools.

all from your phone
Instrument Serif · italic · accent only
Why Instrument Serif

Pure sans reads efficient but impersonal. One italic-serif line per heading adds a human, premium note at the emotional moments. Used sparingly it adds character with no cost to legibility, and never for body text.

JetBrains Mono · tabular · data & labels
Why JetBrains Mono

Monospace makes numbers align and read as real data; tabular figures keep rates stable as they update. Mono-for-data is the convention across trading and fintech tools, and wide-tracked uppercase mono is an instantly readable label style.

05 · Iconography

Line icons, one weight

Thin geometric line icons (Lucide family), a single 1.75 stroke, never filled, never multi-color.

Why outline icons

Outline icons match the engineered, lightweight feel of the type and keep the UI calm. A single stroke weight reads consistent at every size, and neutral color means icons never compete with the data. Lucide is open-source and pixel-consistent, so the product can grow across features without redrawing icons. Green is used only for status and checkmarks.

06 · Imagery

Real product, real hands

The actual app on real devices in real hands, not abstract illustration or stock office photos.

Why product-led photography

Showing the real FlexiWires screen on a phone proves the product exists and works. When you handle people's money, that proof is the difference between trust and doubt. Treatment: clean cut-outs, soft realistic shadows, neutral, mint or dark backdrops, and the device screen carries the only dense color.

Do

  • Use real app screens
  • Calm backgrounds (cream, mint, dark)
  • Soft realistic shadows
  • One focal device per panel

Avoid

  • Generic stock business people
  • Busy or colorful backdrops
  • Hard cut-outs with no shadow
  • Recoloring or distorting the app UI

08 · Motion & layout

Restrained, purposeful

Motion confirms the product is live and rewards interaction without distracting from the task. Layout breathes with large radii and generous rhythm.
