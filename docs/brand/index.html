Brand essence

Precise, fast, premium.

FlexiWires compares every cross-border wire rail on one screen. The brand is engineered and calm with one decisive accent - a signature green that marks the moment of action.

Engineered

Monospace data, exact figures, clean grids. It should feel like a tool built by people who move money.

Calm

Generous space, muted neutrals, a cream canvas. Green is rationed so it always means something.

Expressive

An italic serif accent line cuts through the sans headings - a human note in an engineered system.

Design system

The decisions, and why they hold up

A design system is only useful if every rule has a reason. Below: each choice as a decision, the thinking behind it, and the validation that it supports a precise, fast, trustworthy money product.

01 / 05

Typography

Three roles, three faces: Inter for interface and headings, Instrument Serif (italic) for one expressive accent, JetBrains Mono for data and labels.

Compare rails, lock the rate
Inter · 300-700 · UI + headings
Why

A treasury tool is read fast and often. Inter is a screen-first neutral sans with a tall x-height that stays legible from 11px labels to 64px headlines, so marketing and the in-app product feel like one system.

Validation

Proven at interface scale across modern fintech and tools (Stripe-era, GitHub, Figma). Its wide weight range covers every level of hierarchy without a second sans.

all from your phone
Instrument Serif · italic · accent only
Why

Pure sans reads efficient but impersonal. One italic-serif line per heading adds a human, editorial, premium note at the emotional moments ("routed.", "your phone").

Validation

Sans-for-structure plus serif-for-emotion is a current premium-brand pattern. Used sparingly it adds character with no cost to legibility. Never used for body text.

JetBrains Mono · tabular · data + labels
Why

Monospace makes numbers align and read as real data; tabular figures keep rates stable as they update. It signals engineered, exact, machine-accurate.

Validation

Mono-for-data is the convention across trading, dev and fintech tools. Wide-tracked uppercase mono is an instantly recognizable label style for eyebrows and corridors.

02 / 05

Color

A restrained premium base, one decisive accent. Cream canvas, near-black ink, signature green for action, and hero black for focus panels.

Signature green · #3FE864

Reserved for action and confirmation. Green reads as "go / success / money saved", exactly the feeling at send. Rationing it to CTAs, status dots and the live indicator keeps every green pixel meaningful, and black text on green stays AA-legible.

Cream + ink · #FAF8F4 / #0A0A0A

A warm off-white feels premium and editorial, not clinical; near-black softens harsh contrast for comfortable long reading. The calm canvas lets dense data and the single accent stand out.

Hero black · #111214

Soft near-black panels (hero, corridor rail, app section) create rhythm, frame product shots, and make the green pop hardest where it matters.

Validation

One accent on a neutral base is the durable choice for data products: meaning over decoration, and it scales as the product grows without a rainbow of competing colors.

03 / 05

Iconography

Thin geometric line icons (Lucide), a single 1.75 stroke, never filled, never multi-color.

Why

Outline icons match the engineered, lightweight feel of the type and keep the UI calm. A single stroke weight reads consistent at every size, and color stays neutral so icons never compete with the data.

Validation

Lucide is open-source, exhaustive and pixel-consistent, so the product can grow across features (rails, compliance, alerts) without redrawing icons. Green is used only for status and checkmarks.

04 / 05

Imagery

The real app, on real devices, in real hands. Product-led photography, not abstract illustration or stock office shots.

Why

Showing the actual FlexiWires screen on a phone proves the product exists and works. When you handle people's money, that proof is the difference between trust and doubt.

Treatment

Clean cut-outs, soft realistic shadows, neutral / mint or dark backdrops. The device screen carries the only dense color: the app UI itself.

Do

  • Use real app screens
  • Keep backgrounds calm (cream, mint, dark)
  • Soft realistic shadows
  • One focal device per panel

Avoid

  • Generic stock business people
  • Busy or colorful backdrops
  • Hard cut-outs with no shadow
  • Recoloring or distorting the app UI
05 / 05

Motion & layout

Restrained and purposeful. Motion confirms the product is live and rewards interaction without ever distracting from the task.

Continuous corridor rail

An always-moving marquee of live corridors signals an active, real-time network, reinforcing "12 partner rails, live".

3D button text-roll

A small premium flourish on CTAs that rewards the hover without pulling focus from the next step.

Reveal on scroll

Gentle fade and rise as sections enter, guiding attention and adding polish. Respects prefers-reduced-motion.

Radius & spacing

Large soft radii (32px panels, 999px pills) and roomy vertical rhythm make a data-dense product feel calm and premium, not cramped.
