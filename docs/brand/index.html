Brand Guidelines & Design System

The visual language behind FlexiWires — cross-border money, routed. Use this as the single source of truth for color, type, logo and components across product and marketing.

FlexiWires · v1.0 · Updated June 2026
Brand essence

Precise, fast, premium.

FlexiWires compares every cross-border wire rail on one screen. The brand is engineered and calm with one decisive accent — a signature green that marks the moment of action.

Engineered

Monospace data, exact figures, clean grids. It should feel like a tool built by people who move money.

Calm

Generous space, muted neutrals, a cream canvas. Green is rationed so it always means something.

Expressive

An italic serif accent line cuts through the sans headings — a human note in an engineered system.

Logo

The wordmark

The flexi wordmark with its green glyph. Use the light wordmark on dark surfaces and the dark wordmark on cream/white. Keep clear space of at least the glyph height around it; never recolor the wordmark outside these two variants.

Shape & motion

Radius, spacing & interaction

Large soft radii on panels (32px) and pills (999px). Sections breathe with generous vertical rhythm. Motion is restrained: continuous corridor rail, 3D button text-roll, and gentle reveal-on-scroll.
